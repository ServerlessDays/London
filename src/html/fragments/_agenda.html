Saturday, October 20th

Job Fair

From 10 AM to 4PM the, Job Fair will bring employers, recruiters, and job seekers into the same room for a whole day.
It is a great opportunity to meet innovative companies that are investing in new technologies such as serverless computing.
You will be able to submit your résumé and obtain an interview on the spot or in the next few days.
There will be multiple pitches throughout the day and enough space for job seekers to engage privately with potential employers after evaluating their open positions.

The Job Fair will be hosted at Milano LUISS Hub (right next to the Milano Porta Garibaldi railway and metro station) and is free for everyone!

You can sign up for the Serverless Job Fair here.
