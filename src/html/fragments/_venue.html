Venue

The Conference will be hosted at Fondazione Feltrinelli, in the very center of the city of Milan, less than 5-minutes walking from Milano Porta Garibaldi.

The venue is easily accessible using public transportation. If you're coming by train, get off at Milano Porta Garibaldi FS, then take the exit for Corso Como towards viale Pasubio. If you're coming by metro, tram, or bus, you may want to check ATM's journey planner.

All the Workshops and the Job Fair, on the 20th of October, will be hosted at Milano LUISS Hub, right next to the Milano Porta Garibaldi railway and metro station.
