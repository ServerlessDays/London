Sheen Brisals

Sheen Brisals is a seasoned software engineer, AWS Certified Solutions Architect and a team coach, currently architecting and guiding a team of engineers building serverless eCommerce solutions at LEGO. He has previously worked in engineering and leadership positions with leading software organizations. He is passionate about serverless and managed services, a keen follower of AWS cloud solutions and the architectural patterns. Away from technology, Sheen likes walking and developing his culinary skills.

Talk: Serverless Journey of shop.LEGO.com

Connecting LEGO play experience with millions requires an innovative platform. This fueled the cloud migration of the legacy eCommerce application. This talk will walk you through the principles, the approach, the learnings and of course the serverless technologies that made the vision a reality.
