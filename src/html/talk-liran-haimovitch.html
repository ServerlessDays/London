Liran Haimovitch

Liran Haimovitch is an experienced distributed systems engineer, ex Israeli intelligence officer and CTO of Rookout.

Talk: Debugging serverless with non-breaking breakpoints

Debugging and observability used to be Serverless' biggest challenges, but not anymore, solutions like Rookout empower developers and DevOps engineers to collect data on demand from LIVE code, without restarting, re-deploying or extra coding.

Rookout sets non-breaking breakpoints that collect any data you want and deliver it on the fly wherever you need it, when you need it.
